Classical music often stalls at "sounds difficult." Grader's **classical music assessment** helps you find that very first piece.

## From a mood to a piece

Answer 8 questions, and you'll get a direction for works that suit your current mood. Instead of starting from composers or eras, **you start from "a sound that feels right for you now"** — that's the charm of this one.

## No knowledge required

> Even if you don't know the name, good music still reaches the heart.

There's no correct order for enjoying classical music. Play the piece you meet through the assessment while you work, or during a quiet evening. If you like it, move on to another piece by the same composer — and your world widens naturally.
