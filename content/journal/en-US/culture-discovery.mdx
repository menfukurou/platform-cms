There's so much out there, yet choosing is hard. For moments like that, Grader offers recommendation-style assessments that help you find your next pick from your current mood.

## Assessments across culture

- **Recommended anime (2000 onward)** / **Recommended anime of 2025**
- **Recommended novels (2000 onward)**
- **Recommended J-POP (2000 onward)** / **Recommended Western pop (2000 onward)**
- **Recommended K-dramas**

Each is just 8 questions. Answer a few things about your mood and taste, and you'll get a direction for works that might suit you.

## Make "choosing" fun

> Deliberating is fun too, but a small nudge makes the first step lighter.

Rather than pinning down the one right answer, think of it as **a spark for discovery** — "maybe I'd like this." A good companion for a weekend or a commute.
