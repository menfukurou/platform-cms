You're hungry, but you can't tell what you actually want. Grader's food assessments are there for that everyday "I can't decide" moment.

## For two ways of eating

- **Food delivery for your current mood** — for days you'd rather order and wait
- **Easy cooking for your current mood (Japan edition)** — for days you want to cook

Both are 8 questions. Answer how you're feeling right now, and you'll get a direction for a dish that fits.

## Lighten the small choices

> The daily "what should I eat" is small, but quietly draining. We'd love to make it a little lighter.

Less about finding the right answer, more **a way to notice "ah, this is the mood I was in."** When you're stuck, start with the assessment.
