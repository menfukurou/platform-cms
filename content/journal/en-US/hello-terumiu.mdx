Hello, and welcome. We're **Terumiu**, a product studio.

We've just started Terumiu. We build web services and products, and our first one — **Grader** — carries the theme *"Know yourself, one assessment at a time."* In this first post, we'd like to share a little about what we believe and what we hope to bring you.

## Why "know yourself"?

In a world overflowing with information, the thing closest to us is often the hardest to see clearly: ourselves.

What are you good at? What moves you? When do you do your best work, and which choices feel right? We think we know — yet the moment we try to put it into words, it slips away.

> Knowing yourself isn't about comparing with others. It's a quiet practice of tracing your own outline, gently.

That's why Terumiu wants to offer that chance to "check in" as lightly as possible — something you'd casually want to try, with no pressure.

## Three things we hold onto

As we build, there are principles we never want to lose sight of.

- **Honest data design** — show results with grounds, never overstating
- **Gentle products** — intuitive to use, no expertise required
- **Always sharing** — we don't stop at shipping; we open up what we learn

More than flashy gimmicks, we aim for a quiet feel: something you want to try on a whim, that leaves you a little more forward-looking afterward.

## About Grader

**Grader**, operated by Terumiu, is an open assessment platform for personality, skills, and interests. Pick an assessment, answer a few questions, and receive a visualized result with an explanation.

You can read more on the [service page](/service).

## What we'll share on the Journal

This Journal is where Terumiu speaks up. Here's the kind of thing you can expect:

- New assessment announcements and the thinking behind them
- Behind-the-scenes notes and records of how we improve
- Perspectives and tips that help you understand yourself

We hope it becomes a small spark for facing yourself. Thanks for being here — we're glad you've come.
