Grader's personality assessments now include a series that reflects you through motifs from nature.

## Five motifs, five lenses

Even when the goal is the same — knowing yourself — changing the thing you're compared to changes what you notice.

- **What kind of stone are you?** — quiet, lasting strength and the individuality held within
- **What kind of star are you?** — your influence on others and the way you shine
- **What kind of mountain are you?** — your stance, your scale, the side worth the climb
- **What kind of gem are you?** — polished charm and the glow that draws people in
- **What kind of sea are you?** — the depth of your feelings and your changing expressions

## From 16 questions to 16 types

Each assessment has 16 questions. Your answers sketch you as one of 16 types — not better or worse, just the outline of your tendencies.

> Compared to something in nature, the parts of yourself that are hard to put into words become easier to receive.

Start with whichever motif catches your eye. You may meet an unexpected side of yourself.
