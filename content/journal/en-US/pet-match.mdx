Life with a pet is all about the match. Grader's **dog & cat breed assessment** is a way to start thinking about that fit.

## Considering life and personality

Answer 8 questions, and you'll see a direction for breeds that might suit your lifestyle and preferences. Beyond looks, it's an entry point for considering **how a breed fits your daily rhythm and the way you relate**.

## Begin by getting to know

> Every animal has its own character, and welcoming one carries responsibility. That's exactly why we want to start by getting to know.

The assessment is just a fun doorway. If a breed catches your interest, research its traits and care thoroughly, and let it lead to a match you feel good about.
