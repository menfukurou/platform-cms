The most fun — and most agonizing — part of planning a trip is deciding where to go. Grader's destination assessments gently nudge that first step.

## Two assessments, Japan and the world

- **Your ideal destination in Japan** — for your next trip at home
- **Your ideal destination in the world** — for when you're looking further afield

Both are 8 questions. Answer how you want to spend your time and the energy you're after, and a fitting direction comes into view.

## A starting point for planning

> Once one destination is set, all the research that follows suddenly becomes fun.

The assessment is just a starting point. Let the suggestions send you to a map, or to browse photos. Enjoy that excited stretch of time before a trip.
