Terumiu Operations Office ("we", "us") sets out this Privacy Policy (the "Policy") regarding how we handle information of users on the website terumiu.com (the "Site") and the services we provide (together, the "Services").

## 1. Information we collect

In providing the Services, we may collect the following:

- Access logs (IP address, browser type, referrer, date and time of access, etc.)
- Information collected through cookies and similar technologies
- Information you enter through contact forms and the like

## 2. How we use information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- To provide, maintain, and improve the Services
- To understand usage and perform analytics
- To respond to inquiries
- To prevent misuse and ensure security

## 3. Cookies and analytics

The Site may use cookies and analytics tools to understand usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the Services may not work correctly as a result.

## 4. Disclosure to third parties

We will not disclose collected personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

- With the user's consent
- When required by law
- When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property and consent is difficult to obtain

## 5. Use of external services

The Services may use external services for analytics, forms, and the like. The handling of information by these external services follows the privacy policies set by each service.

## 6. Security management

We take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of the information we collect, and to otherwise manage it safely.

## 7. Requests for disclosure, correction, or deletion

Users may request disclosure, correction, suspension of use, or deletion of their personal information held by us. After confirming the request comes from the individual, we will respond within a reasonable period.

## 8. Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Policy as needed. Any revised version takes effect when posted on the Site.
